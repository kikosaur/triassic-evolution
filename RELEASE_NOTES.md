### üß¨ Evolution & Research
- **Tech Tree Progression**: Unlock over 20 unique evolutionary traits, from "Serrated Teeth" to "Quadrupedalism".

### ü¶¥ Economy & Management
- **Dual Currency System**: 
  - **DNA**: Generated by tapping and your dinosaurs. Used for basic research and hatching.
  - **Fossils**: Premium currency found rarely while digging or earned through quests. Used for powerful upgrades and instant unlocks.
- **Dino Market**: Purchase new species and manage your herd.
- **Museum**: View your unlocked discoveries and learn real paleontology facts.
- **Habitat Upgrades**: Balance your ecosystem by purchasing Vegetation and Critters to keep your dinosaurs fed.

### ü¶ñ Simulation & Behavior
- **Realistic Ecosystem**:
  - **Herbivores** eat Vegetation. If it runs out, they starve and stop producing DNA.
  - **Carnivores** hunt Herbivores if the Critter population falls to 0%.
- **Lifecycle System**: Dinosaurs age, grow in size, and eventually die, leaving behind fossils.
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Beautiful visual transitions as your era progresses.

## üõ°Ô∏è Ethical Considerations & Data Privacy

We believe in transparency and respecting user privacy.

### 1. Data Collection
We collect **minimal data** necessary for the game's core functions:
- **Game State**: We save your progress (Money, Dinos, Research) to the cloud so you can play on multiple devices.
- **Authentication**: If you create an account, we store your email (encrypted) solely for login purposes.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

### 2. No Tracking or Ads
- **Zero Ads**: This game is free from intrusive advertising.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use third-party trackers (like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) to monitor your behavior.

### 3. User Control
- **Offline Mode**: You can play the game entirely offline. Your save data will remain on your device and never touch our servers.
- **Data Deletion**: You can request full account deletion at any time by contacting support.
